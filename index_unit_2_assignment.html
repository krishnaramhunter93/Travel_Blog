Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Colosseum

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in Rome, Italy. It is a testament to the architectural prowess of the Roman Empire and is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom in New York, USA. It stands as a welcoming sight to immigrants arriving from abroad and is a powerful symbol of hope and liberty.
